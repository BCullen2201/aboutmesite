About Me

My name is Brandon Cullen. I'm from the United States, I'm 21 years old, and I started gaining an interest in programming around 2 years ago.

My father was the biggest reason I really started getting into programming. He's taught me a lot of what I know, and always encourages me to learn more.

I first started by learning C# back in the summer of 2020, during the COVID-19 lockdown. I chose C# because I originally wanted to try to make video games using Unity, and there was a tutorial for C# made by Brackeys, who were a channel mainly focused on game development using the Unity engine.

I don't really aspire to make games anymore. I mostly just write code as a hobby now. It's something I'm interested in, it makes me use my brain, and I enjoy it. (Most of the time!)
